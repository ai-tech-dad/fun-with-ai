QUESTIONS

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see your question here, our friendly support team is just a click away.

Is it legal to order contacts online?

Yes, it's completely legal to order contacts online with a valid prescription. As a medical device, contact lenses require a prescription by law. We verify every prescription with your eye doctor before shipping your order.

What if I don't have my prescription information?

No problem! You can simply send us a photo of your contact lens box (the one you're currently using), and we can get all the information we need from that. Alternatively, you can provide your eye doctor's contact information, and we'll reach out to them directly to get your prescription.

How long does delivery take?

Standard shipping is free and typically takes 5-7 business days after your prescription is verified. Need them faster? Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for an additional fee, which can get your contacts to you in as little as 2-3 business days.

My prescription is expired. Can I still order?

If your prescription is expired, you'll need to renew it before ordering. The good news is that we offer a convenient online prescription renewal service for most customers. It takes just a few minutes and can be done entirely from home. The renewal is conducted by licensed eye care professionals and costs a fraction of an in-office visit.

Do you carry all brands of contact lenses?

We carry all major brands including Acuvue, Air Optix, Biofinity, Dailies, and many more. If you don't see your brand listed, contact our customer service team – we can often special order brands that aren't shown on our website.
