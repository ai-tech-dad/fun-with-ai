I Know My Brand & Prescription

You know exactly what you need and want to reorder quickly.

Great! This is the fastest way to order. Simply:

  • Search for your brand
  • Enter your prescription details
  • Proceed to checkout

I Have My Prescription

You have your prescription but aren't sure which brand to choose.

No problem! We can help you find the perfect contacts:

  • Upload a photo of your prescription
  • Our experts will recommend brands that match
  • Choose the option that works best for you

I Have My Contact Lens Box

You have your current box but not your prescription details.

Perfect! Your box has all the information we need:

  • Enter the brand name from your box
  • Input the prescription details printed on the box
  • We'll verify your prescription with your doctor

I Don't Have My Prescription

You need contacts but don't have your prescription details.

Don't worry! We can help you get your prescription:

  • Provide your doctor's information
  • We'll contact them for your prescription
  • Once verified, you can complete your order

Or try our ExpressExam to renew your prescription online!

I Want to Use Insurance

You have vision insurance and want to apply benefits to your order.

We work with most major insurance providers:

  • Enter your insurance details
  • We'll verify your benefits
  • Your discount will be applied at checkout
